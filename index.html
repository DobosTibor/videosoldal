About Me

I am Tibor Dobos, 17 years old.

I started video making at the age of ten with a phone... and I haven't really stopped since.

Back then, it was just curiosity. I watched music videos, followed YouTubers, and wondered: how does a simple moment become something that impacts others?

Today, I work on videography and photography alongside school.

What was once just an interest has become the place where I truly feel at home.

For me, filmmaking is not just recording.
It's Atmosphere. Story. A Moment that can be lived again.

I created this site to show where I am now and where I want to go.

Because honestly: I'm only just starting to take it really seriously.

If you're interested in a youthful, creative perspective that also speaks to my generation, you're in the right place.

And if you have an idea... maybe we'll launch that rocket together 🚀
